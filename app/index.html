O mnie

Hi, My name is Wiktoria. I am an aspiring Junior Front-End Developer. Why? Because this is something that makes me curious. Because I don’t notice time passing by while I am doing it. Because to me success is doing something that matters to me and wanting to learn new things each day. You might say that I’m not experienced. You may look at it that way. But there are many more things that ‘I am’ than ‘I’m not’ I’m curious, creative and hard working. I’m open to new ideas, people and experiences. I am kind, easy to work with and sometimes even funny. You might ask why I am not waiting for a better time - some time in the future, when I might have more knowlegde. I don’t want to wait because whenever I’m doing something else, I still keep thinking about this in the back of my head. When I get home from work I forget to make dinner and it’s already 10pm because I’m trying to learn something new. Because I know that real work experience will teach me more than anything else. And I am ready to take on the adventure.
